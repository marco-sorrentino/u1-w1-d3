Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramalized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

ingredients

For the Focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oili, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired.
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
